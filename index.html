How each volume works

Module by module Every module is a self-contained page — sidebar navigation, plain-language sections, interactive calculators, and a scored quiz at the end.
A running readiness picture Each volume has its own Final Review and Flashcard deck. Every answer, anywhere in that volume, feeds a single Readiness Dashboard — concept by concept.
Independent by design Each volume's progress is tracked separately, since CFA Institute itself treats these as three distinct, standalone readings — study whichever you need.
A companion study toolkit for the CFA Program's three official prerequisite reading volumes — Quantitative Methods, Economics, and Financial Statement Analysis. Not a replacement for official CFA Institute materials.
